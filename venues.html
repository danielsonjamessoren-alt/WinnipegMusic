Venues

The Toad in the Hole

The Toad in the Hole is a Winnipeg staple in the Osborne area. Most nights of the week you will find some sort of live event, accompanied by great food, and a great atmosphere.

High & Lonesome Club

The High and Lonesome Club is an amazing spot to find all genres of music throughout the whole year, also check out their Blue Note patio during the summer.

Kings Head

The Kingshead has many types of live entertainment from local bands to local comedians, located in the heart of the exchange, makes it a great stop for a night downtown.
